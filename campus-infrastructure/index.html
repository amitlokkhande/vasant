Infrastructure Details

Comprehensive overview of our world-class facilities and amenities

Academic Block

Our main academic building houses spacious classrooms, faculty rooms, and administrative offices. Each classroom is equipped with modern teaching aids including projectors, smart boards, and excellent acoustics for optimal learning experience.

  • 25+ Well-ventilated Classrooms
  • Smart Board Technology
  • Audio-Visual Equipment
  • Wi-Fi Connectivity

Computer Laboratory

State-of-the-art computer labs equipped with latest hardware and software to support our Computer Science, BCA, and other technology-related programs. High-speed internet and modern computing infrastructure ensure hands-on learning.

  • 150+ Modern Computers
  • Latest Software Suite
  • High-Speed Internet
  • 24/7 Technical Support

Digital Library

Our comprehensive library serves as the knowledge hub of the college with an extensive collection of books, journals, magazines, and digital resources. Quiet study areas and research facilities support academic excellence.

  • 10,000+ Books Collection
  • Digital Resources Access
  • Reading Rooms
  • Research Assistance

Seminar & Conference Halls

Well-designed seminar halls and conference rooms facilitate academic discussions, guest lectures, workshops, and cultural events. Modern audio-visual systems ensure effective communication and presentation delivery.

  • Multiple Seminar Halls
  • Advanced AV Systems
  • Comfortable Seating
  • Event Management Support
