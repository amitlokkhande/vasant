The organization behind Vasantrao Kale College of Journalism and Computer Science, empowering rural youth through access, opportunity, and affordability.

About the Sanstha

Annasaheb Patil Sevabhavi Sanstha (APSS) is a social initiative focused on lifting talented rural students into higher education. APSS funds and operates Vasantrao Kale College as a concrete step toward building opportunity where it is needed most.

Under the leadership of Chairman Mr. Ramesh Pokale, the college is envisioned as a responsible, community-driven model that integrates quality academics with access to scholarships and student support.

Mission

Break access barriers for rural talent lacking opportunity, exposure, or financial means.

Focus

Higher and technical education in Journalism and Computer Science.

Affordability

Relatively affordable fee structures with active scholarship guidance for eligible students.

Environment

A nature-friendly, disciplined, and secure campus aligned with holistic development.

APSS is a social initiative dedicated to empowering rural youth by improving access to quality education. It is the organization that funds and operates Vasantrao Kale College of Journalism and Computer Science in Beed.

To break access barriers for talented rural students who lack opportunity, exposure, or financial means—specifically providing higher and technical education in Journalism and Computer Science.

The initiative is led by Chairman Mr. Ramesh Pokale, who envisions the college as part of a broader social responsibility mission to empower rural innovators.

The college offers professional, career-focused programs with relatively affordable fees and leverages government scholarship schemes for SC/ST/minority students.
