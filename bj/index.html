Bachelor of Journalism (BJ) Course Structure

Comprehensive three-year program designed to build strong foundations in journalism, media ethics, and professional reporting

01

First Year

Foundation & Journalism Basics

  • Introduction to Journalism
  • News Writing & Reporting
  • English Language & Grammar
  • Media History & Evolution
  • Communication Theory
  • Basic Photography & Visual Journalism
  • General Knowledge & Current Affairs
  • Environmental Studies
Semester I & II
02

Second Year

Advanced Reporting & Media Production

  • Investigative Journalism
  • Broadcast Journalism (Radio & TV)
  • Print Media Design & Layout
  • Media Laws & Ethics
  • Feature Writing & Editorial Writing
  • Video Production & Editing
  • Sports & Entertainment Journalism
  • Media Research & Data Analysis
Semester III & IV
03

Third Year

Specialization & Professional Practice

  • Digital Journalism & Online Media
  • Documentary Production
  • Political & Economic Journalism
  • International Journalism
  • Social Media & Citizen Journalism
  • Media Entrepreneurship
  • Final Year Project & Portfolio
  • Media Internship & Field Work
Semester V & VI

3 Years
